Frequently Asked Questions

What are your opening hours?

We're open Monday-Friday 9AM-6PM, Saturday 9AM-5PM, and closed on Sundays. Bank holidays may vary.

Do you offer free delivery?

Delivery charges vary by location and order size. Contact us for a quote. Some orders qualify for free delivery.

Installation timelines depend on the project scope. We typically complete most jobs within 1-3 days.
